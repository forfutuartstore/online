About Us

Soaring to New Heights in the World of Art

At Forfutu Online Art Store, we're not just selling art; we're crafting a legacy. Founded by the visionary trio of Stephen, Daniel, and Joseph Mutuku, our platform is a testament to the power of art and business coming together in perfect harmony.

A Gallery Without Walls

Our online gallery is a curated space where creativity takes flight. Every week, we showcase seven stunning artworks, each a masterpiece waiting to be discovered. From emerging talents to established artists, our diverse collection offers something for every art enthusiast.

More Than Just Art

But Forfutu is more than just a gallery. It's a thriving ecosystem where art and business intersect. Explore our store for unique merchandise inspired by the works of our featured artists. From limited-edition prints to exclusive apparel, you'll find the perfect pieces to elevate your style and support the artists you love.

Staying Connected

To stay up-to-date on our latest offerings, exhibitions, and events, visit our Updates page. We also invite you to tune in to our weekly podcast on Spotify, where we delve into the fascinating world of art, interview artists, and discuss industry trends.

A Mission Driven by Passion

Our mission is simple: to empower artists and connect them with passionate collectors. We believe that art has the power to inspire, transform, and enrich lives. And at Forfutu, we're committed to making that happen.
